ROOT CAUSE FOUND:

Line 509: .filters,.month-filter{grid-template-columns:1fr!important; ...}
Line 510: .cards,.grid,.dashboard-kpis,.report-kpis{grid-template-columns:1fr!important}

These are in the SAME @media(max-width:768px) block (starting at line 505).
They force:
- .filters to 1 column (causing date to go full row and look weird)
- .cards to 1 column (causing KPI cards to stack vertically)

Later CSS at line 569 tries to override with 1fr 1fr but line 509/510 also has !important.
Since they're in the same specificity, the LAST one wins.

The fix:
- Line 509: change .filters to grid-template-columns:1fr 1fr
- Line 510: change .cards to grid-template-columns:1fr 1fr
- Then the date input needs to fit in its cell properly (not overflow)
